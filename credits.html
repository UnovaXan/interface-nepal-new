Water for Change

Water for Change is a story of hardship experienced by a settlement of flood victims living near the industrial area of Bara district in Nepal. It shows the miraculous change experienced by the community people after the installment of a water tank. This video was produced to be presented at the International Conference on Water and Sanitation.

From Sanischare to Baltimore

The Bhutanese citizens of Nepali origin have been taking shelter in seven camps in Eastern Nepal for nearly two decades, since their eviction from their homes by the Bhutanese government. This video report is about the process of resettlement of Bhutanese refugees from Sanischare Refugee Camp in Jhapa to Baltimore, USA. It captures the process from leaving the camp until they have resettled in the foreign land. Hardship and suffering of leading a refugee's life is shown through this video report
